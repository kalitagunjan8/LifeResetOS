# Life Reset OS - Brand Guide

## Brand Mission

Help people reclaim their attention and become the person they promised themselves they'd be.

---

# Brand Personality

- Calm
- Minimal
- Intentional
- Honest
- Practical
- Modern
- Premium
- Human

The app should feel like a quiet workspace.

Not a social media app.

Not a game.

Not a productivity app screaming for attention.

---

# Design Principles

Less UI.

More focus.

Everything should have a purpose.

No unnecessary animations.

No bright distracting colors.

No clutter.

---

# Visual Style

Modern Minimalism

Lots of whitespace

Rounded corners

Soft shadows

Readable typography

Premium feel
