# Life Reset OS

## Vision

Life Reset OS helps people regain control over their attention, build meaningful habits, and consistently become the person they want to be.

## Mission

To replace mindless scrolling with intentional living by helping users make promises to themselves—and keep them.

## Core Principles

- Simplicity over complexity.
- Real-world progress over app engagement.
- Protect attention, don't steal it.
- Build consistency, not perfection.
- Every feature must solve a real problem.

## Long-Term Goal

Become the daily operating system for personal growth rather than just another productivity app.
